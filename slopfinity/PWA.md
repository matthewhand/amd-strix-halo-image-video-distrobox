# Slopfinity PWA

Slopfinity ships as an installable Progressive Web App for a desktop-icon
experience (precursor to a future Tauri AppImage build).

## Installing

1. Start the dashboard (`slopfinity` service) and open it in Chrome, Edge, or
   Firefox (e.g. `http://localhost:9099`).
2. Click the browser's **Install** button in the address bar (Chrome/Edge) or
   open the menu and choose **Install this site as an app** (Firefox).
3. The app launches in its own window with the Slopfinity icon, pulling
   theme/name from the active branding profile.

Local-only: no cloud sync, no telemetry, no push. Uninstall from your OS or
`chrome://apps`.
